  • About the IBM Challenge

    • Wind Prophecy is a web app meant to support the existing Power Grid by providing Real-Time Forecasts of Power produced by Wind Farms across the 4 major metropolitan cities of India.
    We have designed a Time Series Forecasting algorithm specifically for this task!
    Anyone can use this web app because it supports the Renewable Energy Dream of a Sustainable India.
